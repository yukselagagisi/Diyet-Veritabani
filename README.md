This project is designed to make it easier for dietitians to store data. Pages named clients, actions, categories and files work in conjunction with the clients page. 
Whatsapp Contacts, Tags, Provided Products and Recommendations pages work in conjunction with the Whatsapp Contacts page. You can easily handle your tasks of adding clients, adding clients' files, and managing money flow with this program. 
You can also assign specific labels to people who request support via WhatsApp. You can list the products you give to people on Whatsapp and keep the suggestions you give to these people on this program.
